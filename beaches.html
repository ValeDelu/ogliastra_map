Ogliastra Coastline Beaches

Cala Goloritzè

📍 Baunei | 🏖️ Pebble beach | ⭐ UNESCO site

Famous for its iconic limestone pinnacle and crystal-clear waters. Accessible by hike or boat.

Cala Mariolu

📍 Baunei | 🏖️ White pebbles | ⭐ Best snorkeling

Known for its white pebbles and turquoise waters. Popular with boat tours.

Marina di Arbatax

📍 Tortolì | 🏖️ Red rocks | ⭐ Iconic scenery

Famous for its red porphyry rock formations. Family-friendly beach with services.

Coccorrocci Beach

📍 Cardedu | 🏖️ Sandy | ⭐ Hidden gem

Secluded beach surrounded by Mediterranean vegetation. Perfect for relaxation.
